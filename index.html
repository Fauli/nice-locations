Nice Locations in Zürich

Afternoon coffee

Kafi Paradiesli

Super nice cozy coffee near Kunsthaus!

Cabaret Voltaire

Avant-garde art exhibits, live jazz, plus a hip cafe/bar in the spiritual & quirky home of Dadaism!

Kafi Freud

Hip coffee place with beautiful interieur. Plants all over the place!

Miro coffee Zürich

Simple place with good coffee

sphères

Airy, industrial cafe that doubles as bar & bookstore, offering events, plus coffee, snacks & beers

Afternoon outdoors

Negrellisteg

Cool walk over the rails near mainstation, nice view :)

Polyterrasse

Nice view on the city from the terrace of the famous university ETH

Käferberg

1hour walk from Zürich mainstation, small "mountain" with a very nice view.

Botanical Garden

The small but nice botanical garden uf the university of Zürich.

Afternoon Museum

Kunsthaus

Famous museum with a variety of exhibitions. Good for a cold or rainy day!

Haus Konstruktiv

Modern art museum in a former power station, exhibiting concrete, constructivist & conceptual art.

Löwenbräukunst

Smaller museum. Where beer was once brewed, there are now museums, galleries, publishing houses, art and cultural foundations, art escape room and a restaurant under one roof.

Toni Areal

University of arts and museum!

Evening

Kasheme

One of my favorite chill bars

Only opens 17:00

Gerolds Garten

Cool outdoor area with Berlin feeling

Dinner

Burgstein's Gasthaus Penalty

Typical swiss oldschool restaurant with mainly cordon-bleu.

Schnupf

Famous local small, and crowded, restaurant

Things to plan in advance

Bruno Weber Park

Super artsy outdoor place
